    • Art & Photography

    My artwork is inspired by family, food and shoes –– just a few of my passions. Some of my artwork has been exhibited at national juried art shows. Check out the giant ear of corn morphing out of a recycled 55-gallon oil drum. As part of the Oil Drum Art movement, this prize-winning corn sculpture made its way around numerous Connecticut towns for five different exhibits. In keeping with the food theme, my mixed-media sculpture of plaster-cast lemons was on view at one of Charlotte’s NODA art galleries.
